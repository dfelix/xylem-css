Questions we get at every farm show

What is regenerative farming?
It's farming the way nature intended before we decided we knew better. Cover crops, composting, rotational grazing, less tillage — basically, working with the soil instead of against it. Radical stuff.
Do I need soil lab results to get started?
Nope. Start with a spade and your eyes — visual assessments work fine. Upload lab results later when you have them. We support all major European labs, even the slow ones.
How are carbon credits calculated?
With maths, not wishful thinking. We use verified methodologies aligned with EU carbon farming standards. Soil samples vs. baseline, per hectare per year. Reports formatted for voluntary carbon registries — the ones that actually pay.
Can I use Roots alongside my existing farm management software?
Yes — we play nicely with others. Roots handles the soil and regenerative side. Your existing tools can keep doing whatever it is they do. Estate plan includes API access if you want them talking to each other.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. Month-to-month, cancel whenever. We're confident enough in the product to not trap you. Annual billing saves 20% if you're into commitment.

Your soil isn't going to fix itself.

Well, technically it will — but it'll take about 500 years. Roots is faster. Join 4,200+ farms who decided not to wait.
